Our Company

The Journey of Disena Digitals Pty (Ltd)

The inception of Disena Digitals Pty (Ltd) was fueled by a combination of passion and necessity. As a final year Bachelor of ICT student at the University of Mpumalanga, I was constantly immersed in the world of technology and design. However, the realization that there was a significant gap in the market for high-quality, accessible graphic design and web development services in my community spurred me into action.

In the early stages, the company was just an idea, a dream to combine my love for graphic design and software engineering into a viable business. The journey began with small freelance projects, creating logos, and designing websites for local businesses and acquaintances. The positive feedback and growing demand for my services encouraged me to formalize my operations.

In 2023, I officially registered Disena Digital Pty (Ltd). This was a significant milestone, marking the transition from a solo freelancer to a business owner. With a clear vision and a dedicated approach, I expanded my services to include not only graphic design but also innovative advertising solutions and expert digital marketing. This holistic approach allowed clients to benefit from a comprehensive suite of services, tailored to elevate their brand and online presence.

The support from my university, peers, and initial clients was invaluable. Each project, whether it was a simple logo design or a complex web development task, added to my experience and portfolio. The journey was not without challenges; balancing academic responsibilities with the demands of a growing business required meticulous time management and unwavering determination.

Today, Disena Digitals Pty (Ltd) stands as a testament to the power of passion and perseverance. Our team, which has grown to include like-minded professionals, is dedicated to delivering top-notch designs and cutting-edge solutions. We continue to stay updated with the latest trends and technologies, ensuring that our clients receive the best possible service.

Looking ahead, I am excited about the future. With plans to further expand our services and reach, Disena Digitals Pty (Ltd) is poised to become a leading name in the industry. I am grateful for the journey so far and look forward to many more milestones and successes.

Nyiko Mnisi
Founder & CEO, Disena Digitals Pty (Ltd)
