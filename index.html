Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete a website project?

The time to complete a website project depends on its complexity. Generally, a simple website takes around 4-6 weeks, while more complex websites with custom features can take 8-12 weeks or longer. We’ll provide a detailed timeline during the consultation phase.

Can I make changes to my website after it’s live?

Yes, we offer post-launch support. You can make updates and changes to your website as needed. We also provide a guide on how to manage basic updates yourself. Additional modifications can be discussed and priced accordingly.

Do you provide support for websites after launch?

Absolutely! Our service includes ongoing support for website maintenance, updates, security patches, and troubleshooting after launch. We offer various maintenance packages to ensure your website runs smoothly over time.

What’s the process for starting a project with Disena Digitals?

Starting a project with us is simple. We begin with a consultation where we understand your needs. Then, we create a design proposal, gather feedback, and begin development. Once you’re satisfied, we launch the project and provide ongoing support.
